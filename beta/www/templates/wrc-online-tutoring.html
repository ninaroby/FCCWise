How to access online Writing Center Tutors

The Tutorial Center at Fresno City College has recently started using the Worldwide Whiteboard. The Worldwide Whiteboard is an online tool used to connect students with their tutors. The following video shows students of the Writing and Reading Center, how to access the online tutorial feature for Worldwide Whiteboard.

For help, please call 559-442-8205

If you would like to work on a word document, you will need to convert the document to PDF or email the document to the tutor so he or she can convert it for you. Please ask your tutor about converting your document at beginning of your session. After speaking with your tutor, email your document to onlinewritingcenter@fresnocitycollege.edu. In the subject line, please write your first and last name and Online Tutoring.
