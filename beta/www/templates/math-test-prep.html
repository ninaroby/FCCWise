Math Placement Test Workshop

Math Placement Test Workshop Overview

By attending one of the FREE Math Placement Test Workshops you will:

  • Learn about the different math test that are available to avoid taking unnecessary classes.
  • Have the opportunity to review sample math questions with a trained tutor.
  • Be given additional dates and times to take the placement exam

Benefits of Completing the Workshop

As well as saving time and money, you will be able to take the test again prior to the 3 month wait period.

Target Audience

  • Students who have not taken the placement test.
  • Students who have not taken a math class within the past two years.
  • Students who were not prepared to take the placement test or need to retake it.
