Essential Skills Workshops

FCC is very excited to offer the Essential Skills Workshops for summer 2015 (Four week Session) & The Essential Skills Workshops is a ½ unit non-transferable course offered in the 4 week summer session. The workshops are approximately 50 minutes and are open to all students. However, the ½ unit credit will only be earned by students enrolled in the course who complete a minimum of 8 workshops. We look forward to a successful and productive summer.

All sessions are on a drop-in basis and are absolutely FREE!
