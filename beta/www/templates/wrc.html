Writing & Reading Center

Our mission at the Writing and Reading Center is to assist students with writing and reading guidance. We provide one-on-one tutoring for essay drafting, writing thesis statements, help with reading textbooks and study skills, and many other useful resources requested by students. We believe that any student can succeed if he/she is given equal access to essential writing and reading skills.

WHAT TO EXPECT – FOR STUDENTS

All of our services are free to currently enrolled Fresno City College students. Walk-in sessions are 20 minutes. Call or stop by to make a 25 or 50 minute appointment. Stop by and meet with a tutor during our open hours:
